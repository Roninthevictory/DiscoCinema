Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Privacy is our core principle. We want to be clear about what stays on your machine:

  • No Video Data: We never see, scan, or upload your video files.
  • No Chat Logs: In-app chats are transient and are not stored on our servers after a session ends.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect emails, real names, or billing addresses.

2. Data We Process

To facilitate synchronization, we process the following transient data:

  • Discord User ID: To identify you within the party.
  • Playback State: Current timestamp (e.g., 01:22:15) and status (Playing/Paused).
  • Session Metadata: Room ID and Host ID to route sync packets correctly.

3. Local Storage

CinemaSync may use your browser's local storage to remember your preferred room settings or UI theme. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

CinemaSync operates within the Discord Activity environment. Your use of this application is also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We have no control over data collected by Discord during your use of their platform.

5. Data Security

Sync packets are transmitted over secure, encrypted WebSocket connections. Since we do not maintain a permanent database of user playback habits, there is no risk of historical viewing data leaks.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes via our official Discord community.
